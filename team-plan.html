02 · The Proposal

Two purposeful teams, one unified mission

TIER 2
IAM Services Onboarding & Enablement
The team that ensures every application team integrates seamlessly and that IAM Services operate with the highest quality and reliability.
ONBOARDING
  • Guide application teams through secure, end-to-end CIAM API onboarding
  • Configure and deploy AuthX modules tailored to each team's authentication requirements
  • Own the full AuthX onboarding journey — from setup through go-live
  • Serve as a dedicated support partner for all AuthX-integrated teams
  • Manage weekly AuthX release cadence with zero-disruption deployments
  • Onboard new event-driven use cases that expand real-time identity capabilities
TEST AUTOMATION & RELIABILITY
  • Build and maintain comprehensive automated test suites across all IAM Services capabilities
  • Proactively monitor production environments to detect anomalies before they impact users
  • Own alerting frameworks and health dashboards that provide real-time IAM platform visibility
  • Champion a shift-left quality approach — embedding testing early to reduce production risk
TIER 3
IAM Services Dev & Automation
The team that builds and evolves IAM Services — driving new capabilities, architectural decisions, and engineering excellence across the entire IAM ecosystem.
CORE DEVELOPMENT
  • Deliver new CIAM features and capabilities that protect customer-facing applications at scale
  • Engineer Workforce Connect APIs that enable seamless workforce identity automation/utility across the enterprise
  • Build and evolve the IAM Services Portal as the centralized identity management hub for all IAM consumers
  • Drive Identity Event Bus use case development for both CIAM and WIAM, enabling real-time, event-driven identity workflows
  • Design and implement robust, scalable IAM service architectures that support enterprise-grade reliability and performance
  • Own core automation engineering, shared tooling, and reusable utilities that accelerate delivery across all IAM teams
  • Lead platform architecture decisions and shape the long-term IAM technical roadmap in alignment with enterprise strategy
  • Leverage AI and machine learning to detect anomalous identity behaviors and proactively surface security risks before they escalate
  • Integrate AI-driven automation into IAM workflows to reduce manual toil, accelerate delivery, and improve overall operational efficiency

03 · Strategic Benefits

What this unlocks
for the organization

01
Faster Delivery
Dedicated teams eliminate context-switching. Developers focus deeply on their domain — accelerating throughput, reducing cycle times, and delivering value faster across both onboarding and core IAM Services.
02
Deeper Specialization
Developers build mastery where it matters most — whether that's guiding teams through CIAM and AuthX integrations, or engineering complex IAM Services capabilities and automation at scale.
03
Clearer Ownership
Application teams onboarding to CIAM, AuthX, or Identity Event Bus have a dedicated point of contact. Accountability is unambiguous, escalation paths are clear, and delivery is more predictable.
04
Balanced Capacity
Weekly AuthX releases and CIAM onboarding no longer compete with core IAM Services development. Each team operates at full capacity within its lane — protecting strategic investment while consistently meeting consumer needs.
